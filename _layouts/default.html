Seonghun Oh

I am an incoming M.S. student in Robotics at the Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, and currently a Research Engineer at Holiday Robotics.

My research interests broadly lie in Computer Graphics, 3D Computer Vision, and Embodied AI, particularly at the intersection of geometry processing, neural shape representations, and simulation.

I received my B.S. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Yonsei University. During my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate to work with Hao Su and Xinyue Wei at UC San Diego, Young Min Kim at Seoul National University, and Jin-Hwa Kim at NAVER AI Lab.
